About Me


I am a hard-working mother of 3 amazing kids, ages 12, 14 and 18. I started my career in the restaurent industry at the age of 16, I then went into the medical field for a few years, doing everything from reception and referrals to medical assisting and phlebotomy. When I had my children, I devoted 14 years to staying home with them while I volunteered with local organizations and schools. When re-entering the work force, I started a new career at an electric cooperative in my small town. We serve over 14,000 members and 18,000 meters covering much of Coos and Curry County in Oregon. I have worked at CCEC for 4 years but have recently started a coding bootcamp through the University of Oregon to embark on a new career path, Web Development!
